== Description ==

The Medical Formula WordPress theme is a professional and modern website template designed specifically for medical and healthcare-related websites. It features a clean and responsive design with a focus on usability and accessibility. The theme includes various customization options, allowing you to easily create a unique and personalized website for your medical practice, hospital, clinic, or any other healthcare-related business. With its intuitive interface and extensive features, the Medical Formula theme provides an effective platform for showcasing your medical services, displaying information about your staff, sharing health-related articles, and engaging with patients. It also includes built-in appointment booking functionality, patient testimonials, and other essential features for a comprehensive medical website. The Medical Formula WordPress theme is the perfect choice for healthcare professionals who want a visually appealing and functional website to connect with their patients online. Overall, the Medical Formula theme offers a seamless and user-friendly experience for both website administrators and visitors alike, making it a reliable and effective choice for any medical or healthcare website.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
