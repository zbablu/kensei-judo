This new Judo school is hosted in an immaculate 4500 square foot facility, with a large two thousand square foot Judo practice area. Our Judo mats are top of the line, spring supported flooring systems which allows for maximum shock absorption and thus minimizing potential injuries.

The Dojo also includes a recuperation and rehabilitation area, two changing rooms with showers, and even a children’s area for Judo parents that need to occupy the little ones.
